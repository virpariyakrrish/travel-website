❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I cancel my ticket? +
You can cancel your ticket from the Cancellation page. 30% cancellation charges apply and 70% refund will be processed within 5-7 business days.
How to get refund after cancellation? +
Refund is automatically processed after cancellation. 70% of the ticket amount will be credited to your original payment method within 5-7 business days.
How to track my bus/train live? +
Enter your Ticket ID in the Track Ticket section above. You can see the live location and estimated arrival time of your bus/train.
Women discount offer details? +
Women get 10% discount on all bus and train bookings. The discount is automatically applied when you confirm during booking.
How to get ticket on WhatsApp? +
After successful payment, ticket is automatically sent to your registered WhatsApp number within 2 minutes. Make sure to provide correct phone number.
Payment failed but money deducted? +
If money is deducted but booking failed, the amount will be automatically refunded within 5-7 business days. Contact support if not received.
